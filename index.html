Why are we here?


GET SKILLED

With our high-level coaches and exceptional drills, we take skill development seriously. We transform kids into athletes who are ready to play at an elite level.


BE MENTALLY STRONG

Basketball requires more than just athletic talent and healthy mind. Our strong and healthy mind allows us to work hard through game day itself, helping create meaningful connections while they grow.


STAY HEALTHY

We stress the importance of a healthy lifestyle on and off the court. Throughout their time with us, everyone tests their endurance and conditioning from week to week, staying in good shape year-round.


STAY ORGANIZED

We stress proper time management here at the Maveericks. School is its own priority if basketball ever starts to worry you about school grades, we will not let you play.


BE A TEAM PLAYER

On the court and in life, you must rely on teamwork to be successful. To achieve this goal, we learn the importance of basketball fundamentals and foster meaningful connections with each other throughout practice.


GET COMPETITIVE

We create an age-appropriate competitive environment so that all athletes have a chance to play. This allows for everyone to test their newfound skills and improve their playing style.
