WHO WE ARE

Here, we believe that basketball is more than just a sport. Instead, it is a powerful tool that pushes children
to grow in life and enhances our community as a whole. Accordingly, we provide a variety of unique
basketball programs year-round for all ages and skill levels to enjoy.




Meet our Staff

Founder / President

Mishel Schwartz


Mishel has been passionate about basketball his whole life. He started playing from a young age and worked his way up to represent Toronto at the Maccabi games where he competed with other star athletes from around the world and gained a wealth of experience and knowledge.

Prior to founding GTA Mavericks, Mishel was a prominent key to the growth of Thornhill Thunder where he served as the House League Coordinator and Coach. He also worked in many leadership positions at the UJA Federation where he headed many youth initiatives and received invaluable experience working with kids. Needless to say, Mishel is a skilled strategist and leader with the capacity to grow GTA Mavericks into an elite player development centre.

After multiple successful entrepreneurial ventures, Mishel decided to start GTA Mavericks in order to help kids pursue their passions and talents, on and off the court.








Director of Operations

Jake Ohana


Jake is one of the best up-and-coming coaches that you can find in Canada. He has dedicated his life to the game of basketball and is fuelled by his NCAA playing experience and time training with top talent in the world, including numerous NBA players that he used to play alongside with.

Now, he is committed to passing on his extensive knowledge and expertise to help the younger generation achieve their full potential through basketball. Whether it’s helping someone earn a scholarship, improve their jump shot and handles, or simply have fun with the game of basketball, Jake knows what it takes to help your child fulfill their immediate and long-term goals in life and sport.

If being the best version of yourself is what you desire, then Coach Jake will put in countless hours to help you achieve that.
